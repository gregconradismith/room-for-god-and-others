Look in your own heart. Unless I'm crazy, right now a still small voice is piping up, telling you as it has ten thousand times the calling that is yours and yours alone. You know it. No one has to tell you.

And unless I'm crazy, you are no closer to taking action on it than you were yesterday or you will be tomorrow. You think Resistance isn't real? Resistance will bury you.

--Steven Pressfield, preface to *The War of Art*
