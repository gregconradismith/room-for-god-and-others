The Lord God has given me the tongue of those who are taught, that I may know how to sustain with a word him who is weary.

Morning by morning he awakens; he awakens my ear to hear as those who are taught. [ESV]

The Lord God has given Me The tongue of the learned, That I should know how to speak A word in season to him who is weary.

He awakens Me morning by morning, He awakens My ear To hear as the learned. [NKJV]

--Isaiah 50:4, ESV/NKJV
