In this room for God and others, love Him with all your heart and with all your soul and *with all your mind*. Faith is the evidence of things unseen, not a refuge from what is demonstrable or inferred. Faith is not credulity. Faith is a pearl and an open door, not studied ignorance and a closed mind.
