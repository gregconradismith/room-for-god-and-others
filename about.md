Room for God and Others is a home for essays, poems, and journal fragments written by Greg Conradi Smith.

The site is intentionally simple: a place to publish pieces as Markdown, keep them readable on phones and laptops, and let the writing carry the room. All opinions about public events and whatnot are personal. I am speaking as an individual, not in a professional capacity or on behalf of any institution.
