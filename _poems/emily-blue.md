<div class="verse">
What did the Loch Ness say upon that Wed-ness-day?
He said, “Scotlanders, beware, I’m coming up for air.”
And as the water parted and rolled briskly off his back,
Emily Blue, age 92, had a heart attack.
And while all the other tourists cried, “Run, for Goodness’ sake!”
Her soul at rest, she clutched her chest, and fell into the lake.
And though I know you think it gross, her bobbing up and down,
Her purpose is to surface as the Lock Ness swims around.
