<div class="verse">
Something is more than Nothing.
Nothing and Nothing make Nothing.
Something plus Nothing makes Something.
Something plus Something makes Something else.
Nothing from Nothing leaves Nothing.
Nothing from Something does nothing.
Something from Nothing makes Something Negative.
Something times Nothing is Nothing
And Nothing times Something is nothing different.
One Thing times Something makes the same thing.
Something times Something is Something Squared.
Nothing over Something is Nothing
So Something over Something is more.
While Something over Nothing is undefined
Nothing over Nothing could be anything.
There are no powers of Nothing worth mentioning
But the powers of Something are something else.
Something raised Nothing is One Thing
But Nothing raised Nothing means Nothing to me.
