<div class="verse">
Come, let us be shallow together
And swing in the wild hammock
Of our Father's open hand
Leaning out from kissing kayaks

Lounging in an effervescent spring
Your words are a fiddle's ditty
Joyfully rising to the surface of things
Your tongue dances when you speak

I could watch the creature forever

Learn its delightful habits, noting
Every kernel retrieved from its burrow
Let us blow bubbles

And laugh in the wind
As God lovingly returns
Each prophetic word
And revolutionary act
To our redeemed hearts
Each gently bursting
On your cheek or mine
Except for one

(One prayer he keeps for Himself
He carefully plans its return)
Let us dance before Him

In our ridiculous pajamas
