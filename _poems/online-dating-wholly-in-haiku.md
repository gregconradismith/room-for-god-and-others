<div class="verse">
HER:
Would anyone mind
if I composed this profile
wholly in haiku?
As you may suspect,

I’m a little different
from most girls you meet.
Gifted in music.

If you want to fall in love,
make me sing to you.
Are you self-assured,

thoughtful, witty, chivalrous?
We might get along.
HIM:
I don’t mind. In fact,

haiku is liberating.
Your beautiful voice
chooses syllables

that any perceptive man
would find intriguing.
Already I hear

an echo of your love song.
I am listening.
I would be honored

To learn how to make you sing.
I promise respect.
\

HER isn’t interested right now

She just met someone recently and wants to see where it goes.
Don’t be discouraged. Magic happens when you least expect it.

With millions of singles on Match.com, you could be just a click away from finding someone who’s right for you. Stop waiting. Start searching!
